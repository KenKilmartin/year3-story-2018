To which I replied, "why yes I remember that very well but how did bread become main currency?" He replied "Well people started to go crazy for bread and offering to sell at crazy prices, this is when us insectoids realized if we emerged now with our bread making skills we could rule".

"What we didn't expect was that there would be a global shift in thinking of bread as a form of currency"
